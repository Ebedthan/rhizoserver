# <img src="./rhizoserver/apps/pages/static/pages/rhizoserver.png" width=40em alt="RhizoServer" /> RhizoServer, a biological database of Rhizobiales 16S rRNA 

RhizoServer is a online database containing curated rRNA sequences,
structures and functions of Rhizobiales which is composed of bacterias capable of plant symbiosis and pathogenic bacteria. A prominent feature of theses microorganism is their capacity of establishing symbiotic relationship with plants. This makes such bacteria and fungi an interesting ressource for the study  of interations between plant and bacteria or fungi, plant nutrition and sustainable soil fertility management.
