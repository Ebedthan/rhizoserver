Welcome to the RhizoServer

RhizoServer is a online database containing curated rRNA sequences, structures and functions of Rhizobiales and Glomerocytina, which are respectively bacteria capable of plant symbiosis and arbusuclar fungis. A prominent feature of theses microorganism is their capacity of establishing symbiotic relationship with plants. This makes such bacteria and fungi an interesting ressource for the study of interations between plant and bacteria or fungi, plant nutrition and sustainable soil fertility management.
